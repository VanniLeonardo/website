I recently graduated in Mathematics and Computing Sciences for AI at
[Bocconi University](https://www.unibocconi.it) with 110 cum laude, including
an exchange semester at [École Polytechnique](https://www.polytechnique.edu).
My current research is **TopoEgo**, a first-author project on topology-aware
4D Gaussian Splatting for egocentric video, in collaboration with researchers
at Bocconi and TUM. My bachelor thesis extended the VGGT 3D foundation model
with SE(3)-consistent uncertainty estimation for learned structure-from-motion.
Before that, I led the computer-vision pipeline of an intelligent prosthetic
arm project, worked on medical computer vision at Vision Dental, and on ML
engineering at Accenture. I'm looking for research internships in 3D computer
vision.
